how we work
start

We start the conversation either in person or via Skype. It’s important to talk about your specific needs to understand the core problem you wish to be solved.

specification

Based on our discussions, we formalize your requirements and create a high level feature specification for the desired product.

wireframe & ux

We design the wireframe and the UX based on the specification. This phase involves lots of back-and-forth communication to make sure the core functionalities of your product are given enough emphasis and the product is easy to use.

ui & gfx

Once we are done with the wireframe, we are ready to build the final screen UIs, making sure your brand is communicating the desired message while following the latest UI trends on mobile platforms.

plan

After all the screens have been finalized and all features are set in stone, we give you a detailed time and cost plan for the actual development of the final product itself.

development

We only start the actual development after we agreed on the final timeplan so all parties have a clear picture on the lead times.

user feedback

Before the big launch, it is recommended to do a beta testing phase with users from your target market segment to finalize the product.

launch

We’ll be there for you during and after the launch, making sure everything goes smoothly. Hopefully the product launch is simply the beginning of a long-term partnership!
